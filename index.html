Little Sicily's is a family-owned restaurant that has been a part of the village
of Brice, Ohio since 1975. At Little Sicily's, we pride ourselves on offering fresh,
quality products cooked in brick ovens. We make our dough from scratch using the
original 34-year old recipe. We also feature our in-house lasagna recipe.

"Regulars" and newcomers alike enjoy the friendly, relaxed atmosphere. Our staff is
like our extended family. They will make you feel right at home and are dedicated to
making sure you havea great dining experience, whether dining in or taking away.

The inside dining areas feature interesting OSU, local high school, and other sports memorabilia, which creates a great ambiance. Four large flat-screen TVs, and one medium sized flat-screen TV, make Little Sicily's the perfect place to spend game day. Prefer eating outside? Then try our spacious new patio.

It is a great place to unwind or visit with friends.
